For the equids.

Prep:

  1. Remove all soaked grain buckets from the inside of the metal grain bins.
  2. Add a half scoop of Skin & Allergy to Spaceman's feed bucket.
  3. If the date is an odd day (ex: December 3), add one of the Previcox pills - already divided into quarters - into Hobbes' feed bucket. *** DINNER ONLY ***
  4. Fill the green bucket halfway with chaffhaye for Calvin.
  5. Fill up the rest of Hobbes' feed bucket with chaffhaye.

Feed:

  1. Close the bottom half of the north barn door in Calvin's stall to keep him in.
  2. Give Calvin his two buckets, the chaffhaye and the soaked grain.
  3. Go into Hobbes' stall and shut the divider to keep Calvin in his own stall.
  4. Pour the contents of Hobbes' bucket into the big black bin (he's so messy!)
  5. Give the donkeys their feed buckets at the same time.

Once everyone has their grain:

  1. Give Calvin one hay bag filled with hay from the loft.
  2. Give the donkeys the equivalent of 1/2 - 1 flake of hay.
    • IF THE WEATHER IS NICE, throw it outside next to the water trough and shut the east barn door to keep them outside while Hobbes eats.
    • IF THE WEATHER IS TOO WINDY OR WET, feed them in their stall.

Wait approximately 1-2 hours for Hobbes to finish. Once Hobbes is done:

  1. If you shut the donkeys out, open the east barn door so Hobbes can exit the stalls.
  2. Open the divider between Calvin & Hobbes so Calvin can exit the stalls.
  3. If the weather is nice, open the north barn door in Calvin's stall.
  4. Place all hay bags in the barn isle so that the horses can reach them but the donkeys cannot.
  5. Check the water trough; top it up if necessary.

Prep for next meal:

  1. Take the empty feed buckets back to the grain bins.
  2. Grass Pellets:
    1. Calvin: 1 quart
    2. Hobbes: 3 quarts
    3. Spaceman: 1/8 cup
    4. Spiff: 1/4 cup

  3. PGR:
    1. Calvin: 2 cups
    2. Hobbes: 1 quart
    3. Spaceman: none
    4. Spiff: none

  4. HES:
    1. Calvin: 2 cups
    2. Hobbes: 1 quart
    3. Spaceman: 1/8 cup
    4. Spiff: 1/4 cup

  5. Add water to feed buckets, just enough so that the water covers the grain.
  6. Place the water-filled grain buckets into the metal grain bins to keep them away from mice while they soak.

Water

Make sure the water trough is always full enough for Spaceman to reach the water level.
If the temperature drops below freezing, be sure the heater is plugged in.


TL;DR:

Soak all of their grain for at least one hour, or all day / night is just fine. I usually prep and soak the next meal right after everyone has been fed.

Calvin

  • 1 quart grass pellets
  • 2 cups PGR
  • 2 cups HES
  • 1/2 bucket chaffhaye
  • one hay bag

Hobbes

  • 3 quarts grass pellets
  • 1 quart PGR
  • 1 quart HES
  • Fill his soaked grain bucket with chaffhaye
  • 1/4 Previcox pill every other day at dinner ONLY

Spaceman

  • 1/8 cup grass pellets
  • 1/8 cup HES
  • 1/2 scoop Skin & Allergy powder
  • 1/2 - 1 flake of hay to share with Spiff

Spiff

  • 1/4 cup grass pellets
  • 1/4 cup HES
  • 1/2 - 1 flake of hay to share with Spaceman

Blanketing

No one needs a blanket if it's dry, not windy, and above 20° F.

If the wind is stronger than 15mph, please shut the north barn doors, top & bottom.
Leave the east barn doors open.

Below 50°, above 35°


Is it raining?
  1. Yes: Hobbes needs the lightweight green Saxon blanket.
  2. No: Everyone is good without a blanket!

Below 30°, above 20°


Is it raining/snowing?
  1. Yes:
    • Hobbes needs the medium weight green Saxon blanket.
    • Spaceman needs the small blue blanket.
  2. No: Everyone is good without a blanket!

Below 20°, above 0°


Is it raining/snowing?
  1. Yes:
    • Calvin needs the heavyweight dark blue blanket.
    • Hobbes needs the heavyweight bright blue blanket with the neck cover.
    • Spaceman needs the black blanket with the neck cover.
    • Spiff needs the gray blanket.
  2. No:
    • Calvin needs the medium weight green Saxon blanket.
    • Hobbes needs the heavyweight dark blue blanket.
    • Spaceman needs the black blanket with the neck cover.
    • Spiff does not need a blanket.

Below 0°


It doesn't matter the weather, it's COLD! Please put clean straw bedding in the barn for warmth and place a full hay pillow in each stall.
    • Calvin needs the heavyweight dark blue blanket.
    • Hobbes needs the medium weight green Saxon blanket underneath the heavyweight bright blue blanket with the neck cover.
    • Spaceman needs the black blanket with the neck cover underneath the small blue blanket.
    • Spiff needs the gray blanket.
